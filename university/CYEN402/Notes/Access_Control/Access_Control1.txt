Kinds of access:
Physical access [Keyfob, Card access, Finger print, etc]
Cyber access [Access via a network connection]

Access Control Models:
Attribute-Based [Based on the rights of an authenticated subject/user]
Discretionary [Owner decides who has access]
Mandatroy [A much stricter version of Discretionary Control]
Rule-Based [Similar to Mandatory, but even stricter in some senses]
